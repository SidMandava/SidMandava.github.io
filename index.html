SURGE — CBR600RR Electric Conversion

Close ✕

First step toward a competitive electric superbike platform. Targeting ~25 kW peak from a custom lithium‑ion pack and integrated powertrain.

What I did

  • Subframe to replace ICE drivetrain with EV components; waterjet prototypes.
  • Battery pack design (initial 26s4p P42A, ~1.6 kWh); busbar sizing & BMS research.
  • Motor/controller evaluation, cooling & packaging trade‑offs.

About

I’m a junior at UC Berkeley (Mechanical Engineering) who learns by building. My work spans EV design (battery packs, drivetrains, cooling), embedded systems (ESP32, Raspberry Pi, ArduPilot), and practical fabrication (waterjet, 3D printing, composites). I like turning rough ideas into working machines and documenting what I learn so the next build is better.

Core tools: SolidWorks, Onshape, MATLAB, Arduino/ESP32, ArduPilot, Autodesk Eagle, waterjet, laser cutting, 3D printing.
